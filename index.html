The Rare Combination of
Depth and Breadth

Arrocore LLC was founded on a simple premise: the most valuable technology partners are those who can think rigorously and build reliably — not one or the other.

The principal brings over 20 years of systems engineering experience, a Ph.D. in analytical research, and active production-grade work in the most in-demand areas of enterprise technology: Databricks, multi-agent AI systems, healthcare data infrastructure, and digital strategy.
